Honk

(Asian inspired street food cuisine)

Honk by Pullman, the Pan Asian style restaurant of the upscale hotel is known to elicit guests in Aerocity with street-style cuisine from South East Asia. Serving Street Smart Food is the motto at Honk.

At the signature dining restaurant of Pullman New Delhi Aerocity, the culinary journey is showcased through the Honk Cart traversing the major rice bowl regions including China, Thailand, Vietnam, Burma, Indonesia and Japan. Travelers and locals alike often line- up for good food from the many food carts that line the streets of Asia.

The ‘HONKing’ and ‘HAWKing’ in the midst of the busy and often chaotic streets are melting pots of authentic, flavourful and traditional street-style dishes. From a quick snack to an entire meal, you can often find the most entertaining cultural and cuisine experiences by visiting the many street food carts in Asia. Our culinary artists at the 5-star hotel have researched and extensively traveled to bring native rice variants from these regions along with the traditional cooking styles to bring to your table an immersive dining experience. This rice is cooked in different traditional ways like using Clay Pot and Bamboo Leaf and similar to give an authentic taste of that particular region.

While the food takes inspiration from the street-style cuisine popular in Asia, the presentation and décor are distinctly natural. The menu has been carefully curated to showcase culinary influences from the diverse regions of Asia, primarily Chinese and Japanese with a glimpse of Thai- (Northern, Central, Southern), Hawker Style Singaporean dishes, Traditional Vietnamese dishes and famous Indonesia dishes.

At Honk, special emphasis is laid on procuring the right ingredients like the Soy Sauce from Singapore or the Yakitori, Sushi, Sashmi, Low Fat Tuna like Maguro from Japan, Cantonese, authentic schezwan from China and more to provide an unparalleled dining experience to the guests.
