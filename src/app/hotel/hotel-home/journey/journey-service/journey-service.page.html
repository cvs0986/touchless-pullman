EMERGENCIES AND SECURITIES

At Pullman New Delhi Aerocity, safety and security of our guests and team members are our first priority. Therefore, we encourage you to take a moment to read emergency instruction guidelines, and take note of them as a precau- tion for any emergency event that may occur.

    Upon Arrival in your Room:

  • Check that your luggage has been delivered and incase of any discrepancy, inform the Concierge at 8227 or dial ‘0’.

  • The map at the back of the door indicates the location of the fire exits and hydrants on your floors. Please familiarize yourself with the location of all safety equipments. The hotel is fully equipped with sprinklers, smoke detectors and other fire emergency systems.

    When in your Room:

  • We recommend that you use the safe deposit box available for any valuables. For your own security, do not share your code with anybody.

  • If someone knocks at your door remember to look through the peephole to identify the person before you open it. Please be advised that all our team members wear name badges.

    When you leave your room:

  • Ensure your main door is secure- ly locked. Do not entrust your room key card to anyone.

  • Follow emergency procedures and do not use elevators in case of evacuation.

  • In case of emergency take the nearest evacuation route assisted by the hotel personal and fire authorities. Be alert for public address announcements and the fire brigade’s instructions.

  • In the event of a major earth- quake protect your head from falling debris and stand under- neath a strong structure like a structural beam or wall. Walk calmly down to the evacuation site outside the building once the tremors stop, as aftershocks are likely to occur.

    When moving around the area; it is always advisable to follow the steps below:

  • When using a taxi, take note of the taxi number.

  • Take a hotel card from Concierge

  • Be alert of suspicious persons and activities.

  • Keep calm and don’t panic in the event of an emergency
